Basic Instructions

It's pretty easy to use...

  1. Go to Settings > Plugins > PHP Tweak
  2. Choose where you want these settings to take effect (front-end and/or back-end).
  3. Add, update, or remove any PHP settings overrides.
  4. Save your settings.

That's it! Those override settings will now take effect.

If you want to view the values of your settings, check out the available template variables.
